About Tom

Strength meets
stillness.

I'm Tom — a Chicago-based strength coach and yoga instructor with a simple belief: the best training program is one that makes you stronger and keeps you moving pain-free for life.

My background is unusual. I'm a StrongFirst SFG I certified kettlebell instructor, a 200-hour yoga teacher, and an ISSA-certified personal trainer. That combination means I can help you build real strength while developing the flexibility and body awareness to back it up.

I work at FFC Chicago and train clients who are serious about getting results — without burning out or getting hurt. Whether you've never touched a kettlebell or you're looking to break a plateau, I'll meet you where you are.
