About Alyona

Your daily dose of beauty & mindful movement.

I'm Alyona — a certified personal coach based in Chicago's Downtown, with a passion for helping people discover what their bodies are truly capable of. My background spans pilates, barre, TRX, functional training, and gym coaching.

I train clients across Chicago, New York, and California, and host yearly retreats in Kauai, Hawaii — bringing together a community of people who believe that wellness is a way of life, not a chore.

Whether you're just starting out or looking to deepen an existing practice, I meet you exactly where you are.
